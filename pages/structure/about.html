About Me

Sneh Patel

Hi, my name is Sneh Patel and I am a Philadelphia based photographer currently attending Drexel University. I am available to do photoshoots in PA, NJ and NY area. I specialize in portrait, automative, wedding, product and commercial photography. I also do street photography for my instagram account (@snehphotoportal). You can take a look at the account for more of my work.

I have been doing photography for many years now. I started with an iPhone during High School and I am amazed by what you can capture with a camera. I practiced my photography with my friends and decide to buy my first professional camera (Sony A7iii) in 2020. Over the course of the pandemic, I've been trying to improve my editing and I have learned a lot. Recently, I have been working with portraits photoshoots and plan to expand my photography business to a larger scale, where I can offer a wide range of services. This would included social media marketing, web development, and much more.
